
**BassMap PL - a search engine for drum'n'bass events in Poland and the surrounding area.**

Currently existing applications (Resident Advisor, Shotgun, Dice) focus on techno events and handle international events much better. There is a lack of a centralized event database focusing on drum'n'bass events in Poland and the immediate vicinity.

Currently, fans find their events:

* **on Facebook** - organizers add an event on Facebook and, with good enough advertising, fans find out about the event on their wall,
* **on Instagram** - through sponsored posts or stories posted by organizers,
* **through friends** - parties are sometimes so "underground" that they never make it to the Internet,
* **on portals like Biletomat, Muno** - still, these portals only inform about events for which they directly sell tickets.

BassMap PL is a portal where:

* you will find all DnB events in your area, in Poland, or nearby abroad,
* you will find out where the event is taking place, who is playing, and what the price is,
* you will see the lineup - and using the "Check the artist" button (v2.0) you will be able to listen to the artist's most popular sets,
* if the event takes place outside your city - you will learn how to get there and cheaply book accommodation if needed,
* v2.0/3.0 - a forum for people traveling to the event by car: you can form groups and carpool to parties together,
* you will learn about travel options to major events in Europe (only the biggest ones; more local ones in v3.0) with full travel and accommodation details,
* you will read reviews about events by a given organizer or artist (v3.0),
* using convenient filters, you will find a party tailored to your style (neuro, liquid, dancefloor, jump up).

**Key features:**

* an interactive map of Poland with exact event locations,
* the ability to manually add events - requires admin verification or a verified organizer account (v2.0),
* the ability to listen to a sample of the music that will be played (v2.0),
* the ability to look for a rave crew (v3.0/4.0 - a "looking for crew" type of forum),
* with massive app growth - even collecting points and exchanging them for ticket discounts, organizer merchandise, etc.,
* The "how to get there and cheaply book accommodation" module is the perfect place to plug in affiliate links (e.g., to Booking.com). If a crew from another city books accommodation through your link while heading to a rave, the app earns money for its servers, and the user doesn't pay a single penny more.
